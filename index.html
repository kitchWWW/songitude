Songitude
location based sounds

Songitude is revolutionizing the field of location-based audio delivery. By providing composers multiple musical parameters that may be altered by location when serving audio to listeners. With Songitude, composers and sound artists can map variations in location not only to volume or on/off as with many existing location/audio platforms, but also to tempo, frequency, echo, and a myriad of other filters, effects, and controls.
